Feed the Moose

What is Feed the Moose?

Feed the Moose is a game where you need to feed coockies to animals by shooting them to the animals. If you miss an animal, it is game over. If you accidentally hit a bomb, it is game over. You can choose 4 diffrent difficulties: easy, average, hard and really hard. Can you beat the game?
